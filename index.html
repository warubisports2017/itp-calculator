How Does It Compare?

Players train with FC Köln while earning transferable US college credits through Arizona State University — ASU. This integrated pathway allows players to advance their football development and academic degree simultaneously during their time in Germany.

12–30 credits per year
100% online, flexible schedule
Transferable to US colleges & universities

Credits transfer to most US institutions, enabling athletes to complete the equivalent of a US university year while training in Cologne — often reducing future tuition costs and time to graduation.

ASU Online enrollment is arranged separately. Credit load is flexible and tailored to each player's academic level and schedule.
